About Texas Website Design

Founded in 2015 by a team of passionate web developers and designers, Texas Website Design emerged from a vision to provide premium web solutions to businesses across the Lone Star State. What started as a small studio in Austin has grown into a leading digital agency serving clients nationwide.

Our mission is to empower businesses with high-performance websites that drive real results. We believe in transparency, innovation, and excellence in everything we do. Our core values of integrity, creativity, and client success guide every project we undertake.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Most projects are completed within 4-8 weeks, depending on complexity. We follow a structured process: discovery (1 week), design (2-3 weeks), development (2-3 weeks), and testing/launch (1 week).

What's included in your fixed-fee packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, content management system integration, security features, and 30 days of post-launch support.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer flexible maintenance plans to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. Plans include regular updates, backups, security monitoring, and technical support.

How do you ensure website security?

We implement industry-leading security measures including SSL certificates, regular security audits, malware scanning, and automated backups to protect your website and user data.

Can you help with content creation?

Yes, we offer professional copywriting and content creation services to ensure your website communicates effectively with your target audience and supports your SEO goals.
